Rules~

Everyone knows the rules of tic-tac-toe, but in case you didn't, here is a handy excerpt from wikipedia:

"Tic-tac-toe (American English), noughts and crosses (Commonwealth English), or Xs and Os (Irish English) is a paper-and-pencil game for two players who take turns marking the spaces in a three-by-three grid with X or O. The player who succeeds in placing three of their marks in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row is the winner." (https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tic-tac-toe)

A couple of other important bits and pieces, however are that player one is "X" and always starts, so subsequently player two goes second and places "O"s. We have decided to adopt Premier League-esque rules for scoring, however, so if you win you get three points, if you lose you get zero, and if you draw you both get one point. Best of 300 games officially is the cooler person so this game can (and has) been used to settle major international disputes. We hope you enjoy.
